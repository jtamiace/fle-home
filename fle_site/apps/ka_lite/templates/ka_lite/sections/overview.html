No Internet Required

KA Lite is open-source software, meaning that anyone can collaborate in building it. KA Lite mimics the online experience of Khan Academy and gives anyone the ability to run KA Lite on a local server. You can enjoy many of the features of the online experience of Khan Academy, like watching Khan Academy videos, doing Khan exercises, and tracking student progress -- all without needing an Internet connection!

Over 4,000 Videos and Interactive Exercises

KA Lite offers instructional videos from Khan Academy on math, science, history, economics and matches the common core standards. Download as many as you’d like to see for your installation of KA Lite.

We provide a diverse collection of math exercises for students that

  • Generates immediate feedback
  • Provides step-by-step solutions
  • Works through a point system to encourage continued practice of material.

Customizable for personal, classroom, or school usage

KA Lite has been used individually in various situations, including a home schooling family on their road trip, a classroom full of students in India, and an entire correctional facility in Idaho.

Self-Paced Learning

Students can work at their own pace, so that advanced learners aren't held back from reaching their academic potential, and less-advanced learners aren't left behind.

Individual Feedback

Teachers and parents can view student reports, so that class time can be used to focus on the individual learner's needs, rather than catering to the average abilities of the class as a whole.

Data Transfer

Our online tools allow teachers to assign students specific sets of material directly from a central, online location. Connecting KA Lite to the internet will allow it to sync its usage data.
